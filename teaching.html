Course Plan

I am planning a course on machine learning for spatiotemporal data analysis, covering:

  1. Autoregressive Models for Time Series Forecasting
  2. Gaussian Processes for Kriging
  3. Deep Time Series Forecasting Models (DeepAR, DeepSSM, N-BEATS)
  4. Graph Neural Networks for Spatiotemporal Modeling (STGCN, DCRNN, GWave, IGNNK)
